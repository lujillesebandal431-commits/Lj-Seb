🍽️ How to Make Lumpiang Shanghai

Ingredients

  • 50 pieces lumpia wrapper
  • 3 cups cooking oil
  • 1½ lbs ground pork
  • 2 pieces onion, minced
  • 2 pieces carrots, minced
  • 1½ teaspoons garlic powder
  • ½ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • ½ cup parsley, chopped
  • 1½ teaspoons salt
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 2 eggs

Step-by-Step Procedure

Step 1

Combine all filling ingredients in a bowl and mix well.

Ground pork, onion, carrots, garlic powder, black pepper, parsley, salt, sesame oil, and eggs.

Step 2

Scoop around 1 to 1½ tablespoons of filling and place it on a lumpia wrapper. Fold the sides, fold the bottom, brush the top edge with beaten egg, then roll tightly until fully wrapped.

Use 50 pieces lumpia wrapper.

Step 3

Heat oil in a cooking pot. Deep fry the lumpia over medium heat until golden brown and floating.

Use 3 cups cooking oil.

Step 4

Remove from the pot and let excess oil drip. Serve while hot and enjoy!
